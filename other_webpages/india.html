Rajasthan: Several Jaipur schools receive bomb threat, students evacuated

posted on 08 June,2024 / Lifestyle

At least four schools in Rajasthan capital, Jaipur, received bomb threats via email on Monday, news agency PTI reported, citing police. Students and staff members have been evacuated, and police teams, along with bomb and dog squads, have reached the schools. The development comes 12 days after similar messages to more than 150 schools in Delhi-NCR sparked a scare of unprecedented scale.
